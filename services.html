Legend Plumbing / Services

Services, by the job.

Six areas we work in most. If your situation doesn't fit neatly into one of these, call anyway — most plumbing problems don't read the brochure either.

01 / Water Heaters

Water heater installation & repair

Tank or tankless, gas or electric — we size the unit to the home, run the gas and water lines clean, and set up the venting correctly the first time. No more running out of hot water mid-shower.

Tankless conversion Tank replacement Recirculation lines Repairs & flushing
02 / Water Treatment

Water softeners & filtration

Hard water chews through fixtures and appliances over time. We install whole-home softeners and filtration systems sized to your water supply, tied cleanly into the main line.

Softener install Sediment filtration Reverse osmosis System servicing
03 / Remodels

Bathroom & fixture remodels

Tubs, showers, vanities, toilets, and finish fixtures for renovations — coordinated with your contractor or designer so the plumbing matches the build, not just the rough-in.

Freestanding tubs Shower valves Vanity sinks Fixture finish-out
04 / Commercial

Commercial plumbing

Restroom build-outs, fixture banks, and tenant improvements for offices, retail spaces, and warehouses — built to code and ready for inspection.

Restroom build-outs Tenant improvements Fixture banks Code compliance
05 / Pressure Systems

Pressure tanks & well systems

For homes on wells or with weak pressure, we install and balance pressure tanks and regulators so every fixture in the house gets consistent flow.

Pressure tanks Well pump systems Regulators Pressure diagnostics
06 / Repiping

Pipe repair & repiping

Leaking, corroded, or outdated galvanized and polybutylene lines don't get better with age. We trace the problem, repair what can be saved, and repipe what can't.

Leak detection Full repipes Backflow prevention Main line repair
